Europe Square


Europe square is located in the southern part of Riki, between Metekhi and wine hills , Noah Jordan beach and Metekhi bridge. On the square there is a monument to Davit Sarajishvili, a Georgian public figure and the founder of Georgian cognac production . Popular attractions of Tbilisi , such as Metekhi Cathedral and Darejani Palace , are located near Europe Square . Here you will also
visit the characteristic architecture of Old Tbilisi and the historic hall distinguished by its beautiful paintings . Here is a sample of modern architecture - the Peace Bridge and the Cobblestone Park , where you can relax, have fun, and climb Narikala by cable car. If you cross the Metekhi bridge, you will get to Maidan and you will have the opportunity to visit Abanotubni , Blue Mosque and other
sights of Old Tbilisi . Tbilisi History Museum (Karvasla) is also nearby , where archaeological, ethnological, documentary material, the richest photo collection, folk, applied and fine arts are kept. There are many cafes, bars, restaurants and hotels around Europe Square , where you can pass the time and taste Georgian cuisine. Great views of Tbilisi, colorful carpets and carved balconies make the area around
the square very attractive, that's why there is always a crowd here. The square of Europe and the small cobbled streets next to it are often visited by both tourists and locals to take photos, discover new interesting places, or just to have a look.
